# 🛡️ Safety & Moderation System

## Overview

$BD prioritizes user safety with a comprehensive moderation system.

---

## Reporting System

### Report Types

1. **Post/Comment Reports**
   - Harassment
   - Sexual content
   - Hate speech
   - Misinformation
   - Spam
   - Self-harm content
   - Other

2. **User Reports**
   - Harassment
   - Impersonation
   - Underage user
   - Scam/fraud
   - Other

---

## Blocking & Muting

### Block User

Completely prevents a user from:
- Viewing your profile
- Sending you messages
- Seeing your posts
- Interacting with your content

---

## Content Moderation

### Automated Detection

- Keyword filtering for slurs/abuse
- Image recognition for NSFW content
- Spam detection (duplicate posts, repetitive messages)
- Link analysis (malicious URLs)

### Content Policies

**Prohibited Content:**
- Harassment or bullying
- Sexual or suggestive content
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Self-harm or suicide content
- Illegal activity
- Personal information (doxxing)
- Misinformation/disinformation
- Spam or scams
- Impersonation
- Copyright infringement

---

## Safety Tips for Users

✅ **Never share personal information** - Don't post phone number, address, email
✅ **Use strong passwords** - Mix of letters, numbers, symbols
✅ **Block immediately** - Block users who make you uncomfortable
✅ **Report, don't engage** - Don't argue with inappropriate users
✅ **Trust your instincts** - If something feels wrong, it probably is
